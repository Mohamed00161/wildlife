Meet the Wild

Elephant

The largest land animal, known for intelligence, memory, and strong family bonds.

Lion

Called the King of the Jungle, lions live in prides and symbolize strength and courage.

Giraffe

The tallest animal on earth, known for its long neck and graceful stride.

Zebra

Recognizable by black and white stripes, zebras are social animals living in herds.

Rhinoceros

Powerful herbivores with thick skin and horns, critically endangered in the wild.

Bears

Omnivorous, powerful, hibernates, excellent swimmers (polar).

Kangaroo

Powerful legs, pouch for young, fast jumper.

Chimpanzee

Highly intelligent, tool users, social, playful.

Hippopotamus

Semi-aquatic, aggressive, spends time in water, huge jaws.

Tiger

Solitary, strong swimmer, striped camouflage, apex predator.

Wolf

Pack animal, loyal, strategic hunter, strong sense of teamwork.

Leopard

Stealthy, excellent climber, spotted coat, solitary.

Eagle

Sharp vision, powerful hunter, symbol of strength.

Parrot

Intelligent, colorful feathers, can mimic sounds.

Crocodile

Ancient predator, powerful jaws, lives in rivers/swamps.

Sharks

Apex predator, sharp teeth, excellent sense of smell.
